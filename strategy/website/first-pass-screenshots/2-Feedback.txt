---

Deuterium3D creates cutscenes, concept art, and cinematic worlds designed to live beyond the screen.

The work begins with visual impact, then opens into something deeper: details to connect with, ideas to relate to, and meaning that stays with you after the scene ends.

Every element has a reason to exist. A character, a colour, or a small piece of the world can carry story, research, and emotional weight— inviting them to see something more.

## LOCKED REEL DIRECTION

**Structure**
Six interactive film frames.

**At rest**
Subdued placeholder media.
Bottom-left wording appears like the original image.

**Hover**
A soft cursor-following projected-light reveal exposes moving footage beneath the frame.
The active frame receives subtle flicker, grain, gate weave, and film-border / sprocket movement.
A large hover word overlays the frame and is also affected by the film treatment.

**Click**
Each frame opens its own preview modal.
Cinematic work can open the TRC teaser countdown.
All other categories open their own distinct in-development preview modals.
